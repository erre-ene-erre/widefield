Info: <p>Jānis Dzirnieks lives and works in Rotterdam. He holds a BA in Visual Communication from the Latvian Academy of Arts and an MFA from the Piet Zwart Institute, Rotterdam. His work has been shown internationally in solo and group exhibitions, including Black Market, Kim? Contemporary Art Center, Riga (LV); Sharp Horizon, Nemo, Eckernförde (DE); Midnight Sunburn, Het Nieuwe Instituut, Rotterdam (NL); Waiting For the Next Minute, Kim? Center for Contemporary Art, Riga (LV).</p>
